ABOUT US

ABOUT US

With a passion for creating unforgettable celebrations, Chandrashekhar Aithal, the visionary behind Venkatalaxmi Kalyana Mantapa, has expanded his legacy with Sri Krishna Sabha Bhavana. Born from a deep understanding of the joy and significance of life’s special moments, this new venue blends tradition with modern elegance. Designed to host weddings, receptions, and grand gatherings, it offers a warm, vibrant atmosphere where every event becomes a cherished memory. Carrying forward the same dedication to quality and heartfelt hospitality, Sri Krishna Sabha Bhavana ensures that every celebration is as special as the people it brings together.
