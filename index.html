ABOUT US

ABOUT US

Venkatalaxmi Group is a trusted name with 27 years of experience, dedicated to delivering excellence across hospitality, real estate, event management, and dining services.

With a strong focus on quality, comfort, and innovation, we aim to create exceptional experiences for our customers. Our ventures are rooted in the values of reliability, affordability, and sustainability, ensuring we meet the evolving needs of our diverse clientele.

Strategically located near NH 66, our projects reflect a seamless blend of tradition and modernity, creating spaces that inspire trust and leave lasting impressions.
