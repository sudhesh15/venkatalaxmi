ABOUT US

ABOUT US

Founded by visionaries H. Chandra Shekar Aithal and Hemavathi Aithal, Venkatalaxmi Builders has established itself as one of the most trusted names in coastal construction. Our journey is marked by an unwavering commitment to creating living spaces that transcend the ordinary, focusing on homes that nurture both comfort and community.

What sets us apart is our unique approach to development - we prioritize sustainable living and practical luxury over temporary amenities. With a guarantee of 18-month project completion and a focus on maximizing natural light and essential facilities, we've redefined what premium living means for our residents.
