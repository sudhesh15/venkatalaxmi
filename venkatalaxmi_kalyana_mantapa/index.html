ABOUT US

With 27 years of service, Venkatalaxmi Kalyana Mantapa is known for its exceptional hospitality and commitment to delivering the best for its clients. The hall is designed to provide a clean, hygienic, and luxurious environment to make every occasion memorable. Over the years, the venue has undergone several renovations, ensuring it remains modern and in line with current trends.

Celebrate in style with Shrilatha Aithal, the beloved host who brings warmth and unforgettable moments to your Sangeet or special occasions. Renowned for top-notch wedding services, this spacious venue is perfect for grand celebrations. With modern facilities and seamless event management, it guarantees a memorable experience.
